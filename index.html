Rules

• Your age rises over time. If it reaches 100, you lose.

• Collect all Young Yums in a level before the finish counts.

• Coins are for the shop only.

• Touching enemies makes you older.

• Stomping enemies makes you younger.

• Gray armored enemies need 2 stomps.

• On iPhone, use the touch buttons at the bottom.

• In the boss arena, aging freezes while the boss is alive.

Boss Fight

• Grandpa of Doom slams the ground and sends shockwaves.

• He summons mini enemies during the fight.

• At half health he transforms into phase 2 rage mode.

• You must defeat him to clear level 6.
